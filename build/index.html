The Revolute is a Smart Dial/Scroll Wheel Device that allows users to perform various actions through simple rotation between the fingertips, making it easier to manage complex tasks/software applications without constantly clicking back and forth with a mouse or keyboard.

Li-ion Battery Powered the Revolute is powered by a rechargable 3.7v lithium ion coin cell battery with the capacity of 70mAh, making the Revolute a rechargable and repairable device.

Wireless system a single circuit board the size of a coin contains all the funtionalities of the Revolute. Utilizing the nRF52833 SoC by Nordic Semiconductors, the Revolute is able to operate power efficiently while establishing BLE connections and maintaining the entire system of sensors and circuits.

Attachments for the Revolute different attachemts bases allow the Revolute to be attached and used on different devices and surfaces, making it intuitive to use for a wide range of applications.

Magtic Virtual-Idents 36 Precision-Placed Magnets provides a increment snap feel, creating a controlled Single Stepping mechanism for the Revolute.
